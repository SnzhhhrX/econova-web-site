— WHO WE ARE

A new generation of builders for the planet

We are students, dreamers, and doers who believe technology and ecology are not opposites — but allies. EcoNova brings together young minds from every corner of the world to build a future that's both intelligent and kind.

— OUR MISSION

Inspire students to combine creativity, technology and sustainability.

We empower the next generation to think beyond borders, design with empathy, and act with urgency. Through education, community, and innovation, we turn climate anxiety into climate action.

— OUR DEPARTMENTS

How we make impact — together

Marketing & Social Media

Manages social media channels and creates visual and written content to share EcoNova's mission with the world.

External Relations & Global Community

Connects with international students, youth communities, and networks like Erasmus+ to build global partnerships and keep the community active.

Project & Education

Designs ecology and technology-focused online events, interactive workshops, and the content for participants' certificates.

Green Tech & Idea Hunters

Researches and explores innovative eco-friendly tech trends, AI tools, and creative digital solutions to share with the team and community.

Technology & Web Department

Maintains and updates our website while developing the technical infrastructure for future digital projects.

Human Resources & Onboarding

Welcomes new international volunteers, helps them settle into their departments, and ensures friendly, smooth coordination within the team.

— OUR FOCUS

Three pillars. One planet.

Technology

Harnessing AI, data and modern engineering to solve real environmental challenges.

Ecology

Grounding every project in respect for ecosystems, biodiversity, and future generations.

Collaboration

Uniting international students, mentors and partners around a shared purpose.
